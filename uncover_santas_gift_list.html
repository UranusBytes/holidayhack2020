There is a photo of Santa's Desk on that billboard with his personal gift list. What gift is Santa planning on getting Josh Wright for the holidays? Talk to Jingle Ringford at the bottom of the mountain for advice.

A Strange Billboard
Billboard Near Entrance
 

At the Entrance near the gondola is an interesting looking billboard. Clicking on the billboard brings forth a fullsize image of the billboard. Other than the amazing enimga machine on display in the image, the item of note is that the wishlist on the desk has been swirled.

Billboard at Entrance
 

From here, I fired up GIMP and started Googling to figure out which filter I could use to unswirl the section of the image. After a couple of trys at selected and using the Whirl and Pinch distoration, I was able to see the following gift list:

Unswirled Billboard
 
  • ed - Two Front Teeth
  • Ian - OU Jersey
  • Jeremy - Blanket
  • Brian - Lei
  • Josh Wright - proxmark
  • Clay - Darth Vader Suit
  • Tad - Holiday Lights
  • Phil - Stuffed Pikachu
  • Jerry - Trip to North Pole

There are some interesting items on that wish list! I will remember these, as I have a feeling they will come in handy at a later time... But for now, I just need to know what Josh Wright is getting from Santa.
