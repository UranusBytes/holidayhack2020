Entry: SHHC20
Entry
 

The Entry is at the front of the castle, accessible from the Entrance, Dining Room, and Great Room.

You can talk with Ginger Breddie, Sparkle Redberry, Piney Sappington, and Santa.

The challenge for Santavator is here.

Hex Nut
Hex Nut
 

On the right side of the room, beside Ginger Breddie, can be found a hex nut.

Santa Portrait
Santa Portrait
 

At the top of the room, behind Santa, can be found a portrait of Santa. This portrait looks very similar to the Billboard near the Entrance

An overview of all people, places, and events can be learned in the Introduction.
