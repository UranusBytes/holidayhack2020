Hidden Room
 

In the Workshop is a locked door. After opening the door through the Open HID Lock challenge, I find a small, dark room with two points of light at the far end.

When I get closer to the lights, I find that I'm looking at the Entry.

But wait... I'm not seeing the Entry through the Santa Portrait. I'm seeing the Entry through Santa's eyes. I am Santa!!!!

An overview of all people, places, and events can be learned in the Introduction.
