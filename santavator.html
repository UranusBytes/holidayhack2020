Santavator: SHHC20
Santavator in Entry
 

In the Entry near Sparkle Redberry is the ground floor door for the Santavator.

Talking with Sparkle, he asks for your help with the Santavator not working, gives you a key to allow you to access the service panel, and mentions that you will need items from around the property to fix it.

Inside Santavator
 

Once you enter the door to the Santavator, you see the service panel.

Service Panel
 

When you initially access the service panel, you will see the workshop button is missing.

Inside Service Panel
 

If you use the elevator panel key to open the service panel, you will some of the various items you have found on the property.

At this point, I had already found:

  • The Broken Candycane from the Castle Approach
  • The Green Bulb from the Courtyard
  • A Hex Nut from the Entry
  • Another Hex Nut from the Dining Room
Talks
Access Talks
 

To access the Talks floor, I needed to convert the white light from the bottom, convert it to green, and get it into the green entry at the top right.

To do this, I used the two nuts and green bulb, though I am sure there are other arrangements that will achieve the necessary green light.

On this floor, I found multiple other items that are used in the service panel:

  • The Red Bulb from the Talks Lobby
  • The Elevator 1.5 Button from the Speaker UNpreparedness Room
  • The Portals from the Release the Snacken in the Speaker UNpreparedness Room
Workshop
Access Workshop
 

To access the Workshop floor, I needed to convert the white light from the bottom, convert it to green, and get it into the green entry at the top right.

To do this, I used the two nuts and green bulb, though I am sure there are other arrangements that will achieve the necessary green light.

Santa's Office
Access Office
 

To access Santa's Office, I needed to also get yellow to the yellow entry. The the additional items I had found in the workshop and NetWars roof, I was able to.

To do this, I used the broken candy cane to divert some white from the primary stream, colored it green with the green bulb, then redirected using a hex nut.

Full Access
 

At this point, all of the Santavator buttons are working...

But... The Santa's Office requires a biometric fingerprint scan.
