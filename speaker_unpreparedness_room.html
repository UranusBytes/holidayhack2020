Speaker Unpreparedness Room
 

The speaker UNpreparedness room is on the left side of the talks floor of the castle, accessible from the Talks Lobby.

You can talk with Tangle Coalbox, Morcel Nougat, and Release the Snacken.

The challenge for Snowball Fight is here.
