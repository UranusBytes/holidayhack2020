Open HID Lock: SHHC20
HID Lock
 

In the Workshop is a door that is locked by a HID.

In the Speaker Unpreparedness Room is Shinny Upatree mentions that Santa would like us to test out the locked HID door in the Entry.

In the Kitchen is Fitzy Shortstack who mentions that Santa really trusts Shinny Upatree.

Shinny Upatree
 

Shinny is in the Castle Approach. So I went to the Castle Approach and stood right behind Shinny.

Proxmark3 Read
 

While standing behind Shinny, I opened the CLI interface for the Proxmark3 that I found in the Workshop.

In the CLI interface, I did a search for nearby low frequency cards by running the command lf search.

Jackpot!

Proxmark3 Sim
 

I headed back up to the workshop and stood beside the HID locked door.

In the CLI interface of the proxmark3, I tried simulating the card I'd read from Shinny by running the command lf hid sim -r 2006e22f13.

Click! The door was unlocked!
