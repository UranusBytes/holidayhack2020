Courtyard
 

The courtyard is at the back of the castle, accessible from both the Dining Room and Great Room.

In the courtyard are vendors booths for Google, SANS, Splunk, and swag.

You can talk with Sugarplum Mary, Jack Frost, and Bubble Lightington.

The challenges for Linux Primer and Santa Shop are here.

Green Bulb
 

In the upper left corner of the courtyard can be found a green light bulb.

An overview of all people, places, and events can be learned in the Introduction.
